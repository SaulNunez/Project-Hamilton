    Emergency

    Handles emergency logic, like activating buttons to turn emergency types. Also handles wait to activate, and progress; offers countdown event for clients.

    Emergencies have three states:

    1. Waiting to activate
    2. After voting, there's a wait before them being available
    3. When a player uses them there's another wait before being available
    4. Available (assasins can activate them)
    5. In progress, when an assasin used them, players are currently scrambling to solve it. Failure to do so finishes the game with victory to the assasins.
